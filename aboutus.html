About Us


Wright Anything Agency was founded in 2013 by Mia Fey and her understudy Phoenix Wright originally under the name of "Fey & Co. Law Offices". After the tragic death of Mia Fey, Phoenix Wright assumed control of the offices by Mia Fey's wishes, and the firm became known as "Wright & Co. Law Offices". Three years later while Wright was unable to work, the firm, under the authority of Trucy Wright, became a talent agency under the name, "Wright Talent Agency." After the return of Wright, the talent agency was reestablished and it is now known as the "Wright Anything Agency."
